open stats ware

![Hex Sticker for the openstatsware Working Group](https://www.openstatsware.org/sticker/openstatsware-hex.svg)

Welcome to the home page of the openstatsware working group. We are a [scientific working group](https://community.amstat.org/biop/workinggroups/swe-wg) in the [American Statistical Association (ASA) Biopharmaceutical Section (BIOP)](https://community.amstat.org/biop/), as well as a [European Special Interest Group (SIG)](https://www.psiweb.org/sigs-special-interest-groups/software-engineering) sponsored by [Statisticians in the Pharmaceutical Industry (PSI)](https://www.psiweb.org/about-us) and the [European Federation of Statisticians in the Pharmaceutical Industry (EFSPI)](https://efspi.org/).

To learn more about the working group see the [about us](https://www.openstatsware.org/goals.html) page. We thank all our [sponsors](https://www.openstatsware.org/sponsors.html) for their generous support.
