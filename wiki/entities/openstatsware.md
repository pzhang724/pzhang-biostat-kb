# openstatsware

## Organization

**Full name**: openstatsware (Statistical Software Engineering Working Group)  
**Type**: Cross-continental working group  
**US affiliation**: ASA Biopharmaceutical Section (BIOP) scientific working group  
**European affiliation**: PSI and EFSPI Special Interest Group (SIG)  
**Website**: https://www.openstatsware.org  
**GitHub**: openpharma organization

### Purpose

openstatsware promotes best practices for statistical software engineering in pharmaceutical research. It focuses on the engineering quality of R packages (and statistical software generally) rather than the statistical validity of the methods — complementing [[R Validation Hub]]'s risk-based package assessment with upstream engineering discipline.

### Notable Characteristics

- **Dual affiliation** (ASA BIOP + PSI/EFSPI) provides credibility with both US and European regulators
- Workshops are publicly available and teach practical R package engineering workflows aligned with GAMP 5 and GxP requirements
- The HTA workstream extends the group's scope into indirect treatment comparisons and health economic modeling
