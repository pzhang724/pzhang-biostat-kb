# Paradigm Health — FDA Real-Time Review Collaboration (2026)

## Summary

Paradigm Health (AI-powered clinical trial platform, incubated by ARCH Venture Partners) announced a landmark collaboration with the FDA for real-time continuous regulatory review. The model captures data directly from EHRs, algorithmically evaluates FDA-defined data points in real time, and transmits only critical safety/efficacy signals to sponsors and FDA — rather than waiting for end-of-study data packages. Already operational in Phase 2 (AstraZeneca) and Phase 1b (Amgen) trials at MD Anderson and UPenn Perelman School of Medicine.

## Key Points

- **Platform**: automates data collection/analysis from EHR and unstructured sources; transmits audit-ready, privacy-protecting signals
- **Goal**: reduce monitoring burden, data entry, trial costs; speed regulatory review; enable continuous (not batch) regulatory environment
- **Participants**: Amgen (Phase 1b), AstraZeneca (Phase 2) as first sponsors; FDA as collaborating regulator
- **Infrastructure built from early 2026**: FDA and Paradigm Health co-defined reporting protocols, validation criteria, and software interoperability requirements
- **Strategic context**: addresses lag between therapeutic volume and trial infrastructure; directly supports FDA's goal of moving to a real-time, continuous regulatory environment
