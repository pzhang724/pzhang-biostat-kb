## Summary

openstatsware is a cross-organizational working group for statistical software engineering, operating simultaneously as a scientific working group within the ASA Biopharmaceutical Section (BIOP) and as a European Special Interest Group (SIG) under PSI and EFSPI. Its focus is promoting best practices for developing, reviewing, and maintaining statistical software used in pharmaceutical research. The group produces workshops, guidelines, and curates open-source R packages under the openpharma umbrella.

## Key Points

- **Dual affiliation**: ASA BIOP (US) and PSI/EFSPI (Europe), giving it cross-Atlantic credibility in regulatory pharma settings
- **Workshop series**: GSWEP4R (Good Software Engineering Practice for R Packages) — multi-module workshop covering workflows, quality, testing, publication
- **Package curation**: openpharma GitHub organization hosts packages developed under openstatsware standards
- **Recent outputs**: `{pmrm}` package (Protocol Monitoring Resource Management) joined openpharma and reached CRAN (Feb 2026); HTA-R Pharma workstream released `maicplus` v0.1 (matching-adjusted indirect comparison)
- **HTA extension**: HTA-R Pharma workstream focuses on health technology assessment methods in R
