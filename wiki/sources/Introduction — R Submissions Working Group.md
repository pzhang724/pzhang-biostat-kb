## Summary

The homepage of the R Consortium Submissions Working Group, showing the expanded seven-pilot timeline through 2026+. Pilots 6 and 7 (both launched 2026) extend the prior work into AI-assisted analysis and synthetic clinical data respectively. Mission remains: make R-based clinical trial regulatory submissions easier today (examples) and tomorrow (influencing standards).

## Key Points

- **Seven pilots total** (as of 2026): Pilots 1–5 completed/active, Pilots 6 and 7 launched in 2026
- Pilot 6 (2026–): focus area not yet detailed in this source; likely AI-assisted analysis based on progression
- Pilot 7 (2026–): focus area not yet detailed; likely synthetic clinical data
- Mission: two-track — show working examples now AND influence future FDA/industry standards
- Public hex stickers for each pilot signal the working group's community-facing, open-source culture
