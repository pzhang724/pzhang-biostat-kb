## Summary

A work-in-progress online book teaching how to use Python to plan, build, and automate clinical study reports (CSRs) for regulatory submission. Follows ICH E3 guidance on CSR structure. Presented as a workshop at R/Pharma 2025 (video available) and as a short course at PharmaDS 2026.

## Key Points

- Focuses on the full CSR workflow: data, analyses, tables/figures/listings, and regulatory submission packaging
- Small, reusable examples throughout; designed for both Python newcomers and experienced clinical programmers
- ICH E3 used as the structural framework for CSR organization
- Represents the growing Python presence alongside R in clinical reporting (complement to Pharmaverse/R tools)
